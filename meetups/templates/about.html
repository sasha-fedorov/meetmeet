Connecting Communities

MeetMeet is a simple, no-nonsense platform for hosting and joining local gatherings.

Organize

Create a meetup in seconds. Set the time, location, and whether you want to approve guests manually or keep it open for everyone.

Discover

Browse upcoming events in your area. From tech talks to hiking groups, there's always something happening on MeetMeet.

Manage

Keep your events safe with host-only moderation. Approve requests and manage your guest list with a single click.

What can you use MeetMeet for?

  • Tech Meetups: Share knowledge and network with local developers.
  • Hobby Groups: Book clubs, board game nights, or photography walks.
  • Study Sessions: Find peers to tackle that new course or project with.
  • Social Gatherings: Coffee chats, happy hours, or community dinners.
